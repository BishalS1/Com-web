About MAN

We are a professional organization of mathematicians in Nepal that aims to promote and advance the study of mathematics in the country. Established in 1979, MAN has been a vital force in fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing among mathematicians. Our association provides support and resources to students and educators in Nepal, helping to nurture a new generation of mathematicians and researchers. One of the ways we accomplish our goals is by organizing mathematical seminars and conferences. These events provide a platform for mathematicians to share their research, insights and ideas, and encourage students to explore new mathematical concepts.

message from founder

As the president of the mathematical association, I am honored and thrilled to serve in this role. Our association is dedicated to promoting the study and advancement of mathematics, and I am committed to continuing this mission during my tenure as president. I look forward to working with all of you to organize events, lectures, and workshops that will foster a love of mathematics in students and professionals alike. We will also strive to increase the public understanding of the importance of mathematics in everyday life, and to advocate for the support of mathematical research.

MATHEMATICAL ASSOCIATION OF NEPAL(MAN)

The Mathematical Association of Nepal (MAN) is a professional organization of mathematicians in Nepal that aims to promote and advance the study of mathematics in the country. Established in 1979, MAN has played a key role in fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing among mathematicians, as well as providing support and resources to students and educators.

OUR WORKS

CONDUCTING DMO

The District Math Olympiad (DMO) is a math competition held in districts across Nepal to select the best math students to represent the country at the International Mathematical Olympiad.

SELECTION FOR IMO

To represent Nepal at the International Mathematical Olympiad, students must first compete in the District Math Olympiad and pass through several rounds of selection, including the National Math Olympiad. The top-performing students are then chosen to represent Nepal at the IMO.

MATHEMATICAL SEMINAR

MAN regularly organizes mathematical seminars to promote the study of mathematics and provide a platform for mathematicians to share their research and insights.
